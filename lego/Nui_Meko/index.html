Nui-Meko

It's scales stand on end when it feels threatened. Nui-Meko are in fact fearsome hunters in their own right. They blend in with the surroundings of the Fau Swamp while they await prey.

This was a fun one for me to finaly get finished, the scailes are an idea that I've had for quite some time. My initial plan was to have a full gradient of colours from the head down to the end of the tail. While I still think this is a super interestign idea, the problem that I ran into was the limited colour palet of part 90640 which makes up the scails. I tested using longer and shorter shells like 90641 and 90639 but wasn't happy with the results.
